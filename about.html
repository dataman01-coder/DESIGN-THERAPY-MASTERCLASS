Our Mission

Graphic Design Therapy was created to demystify professional design and make it accessible to everyone. We believe that great design shouldn't be limited to those with expensive software or years of formal education.

Through practical, hands-on training focused on flyer-based design, we empower entrepreneurs, freelancers, and creative minds to produce stunning marketing materials that drive real results.

Our therapeutic approach combines proven design principles with confidence-building techniques, helping you overcome creative blocks and unleash your inner designer.
